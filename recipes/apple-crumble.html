Apple Crumble

Description

Apple crumble is a tasty dessert consisting of apple sauce and crunmbly pastry baked together in and oven.

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 575g Bramley apples, peeled, cored and sliced to 1cm thick
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar

For the crumble

  • 175g plain flour
  • 110g golden caster sugar
  • 110g cold butter
