Apple Crumble

Description

Apple crumble is a tasty dessert consisting of apple sauce and crunmbly pastry baked together in and oven.

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 575g Bramley apples, peeled, cored and sliced to 1cm thick
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar

For the crumble

  • 175g plain flour
  • 110g golden caster sugar
  • 110g cold butter

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 190C/ 170C fan? gas 5.
  2. Toss the apples with 2 tbsp of golden caster sugar and put in a 23cm round baking dish at least 5cm deep. Flatten down with your hand to preventtoo much crumble falling through.
  3. Put 175g plain flour and 110g golden caster sugar in a bowl with a pinch of salt.
  4. Slice in 110g cold butter and rub in with your fingertips until the mixture resembles moist breadcrumbs.
  5. Pour the crumb mix over the apples to form a pile in the centre, then use a fork to even out.
  6. Gently press the surface with the back of the fork so the crumble holds together and goes crisp, then lightly drag the fork over the top for a decorative finish.
  7. Set on a baking tray and bake for 35-40 mins, until the top is golden and the apples are very soft. Leave to cool for 10 mins before serving.
