Tomato soup

Description

This warming vegan soup is made using juicy, ripe tomatoes, which come into season around September. It's a comforting recipe to make and eat throughout the year.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg ripe tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 small carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp tomato puree
  • pinch of sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1.2 litres hot vegtable stock

Steps

  1. Wash and cut tomatoes, slice off and hard cores.
  2. Peel and chop onion and carrot. Chop celery.
  3. Heat olive oil, then add onion, carrot and celery. Cook for about 10 mins, ocassionally stirring.
  4. Stir in tomato puree, then add tomatoes, sugar and black pepper.
  5. Add in 2 bay leaves, then put the lid on the pan and cook on ow heat for ten mins.
  6. Pour in the stock while mixing well, then turn up the heat until the mixtrue bubbles strongly. Then trun the heat back down, cover with lid and cook gently for 25 mins.
  7. Remove the bay leaves, the ladle the soup into a blender and blend until smooth.
  8. Season to taste and serve.
