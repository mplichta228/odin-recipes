Lasagna

Lasagna is a pasta dish, made from alternating layers of meat, pasta and bechamel sauce. Once the layers are assembled, the dish is cooked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 750mg lean beef mince
  • 90g prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 300g fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g mozzaralla cheesa
