Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a pasta dish, made from alternating layers of meat, pasta and bechamel sauce. Once the layers are assembled, the dish is cooked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 750mg lean beef mince
  • 90g prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 300g fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g mozzaralla cheesa

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a frying pan and cook the beef in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  2. Finely chop prosciutto then stir in to meat.
  3. Add passata and hot beef, then season to taste.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer for 30 mins.
  5. Pre-heat oven to 180C/160C fan, and lightly oil an ovenproof dish.
  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, cover with some lasagna sheets, then drizzle over roughly 130g of white sauce.
  7. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta, and cover with the remaining white sauce until no pasta pokes through.
  8. Scatter torn up mozzarella on top.
  9. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
